How this started

I see broken
processes and
physically itch
to fix them.

I've been in commercial real estate long enough to know it runs on talented people doing things the hard way. I started building AI tools to fix that. Not because anyone asked me to. Because I walked into the same broken process one too many times and thought: this could be fixed in an afternoon.

I'm not a tech person who learned real estate. I'm a real estate person who learned to build. CRE is where I've proven the thesis. It won't be the only place I test it.
